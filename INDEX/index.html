Our History


Home sweet home, there’s no place like it!


Klassy is the brand name under which the world knows Kottakkal Wood Complex, established in 1985.
Its world-class furniture brought it into a position of professional leadership within a very short span of time.

By the last 37 years, Klassy developed a unique quality management system (QMS) for premium wooden furniture manufacturing.
Klassy erected sophisticated and high precision machinery which enabled the company to bring utmost perfection out of each products.
More than 1000 unique designs were conceived and developed by our designing team during the course of these years.
The designs align with the modern pattern while retaining within them the essence of antique and traditional styles.
The striving for perfection has always been the sole motive while producing furniture ever since the inception of Klassy.
